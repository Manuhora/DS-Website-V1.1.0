Discover an exceptional educational journey at Discovery School, an international, Nursery - 12th grade college preparatory school. A school community where all are inspired to learn, empowered to lead positively, and challenged to excel.

Global citizens with
leadership skills

We’re committed to shaping future leaders by equipping our students with the skills to thrive in a global, ever-changing world. Through a world-class education, we focus on critical thinking, effective communication, and cross-cultural understanding.

College Prep

Discovery School alumni thrive in colleges and universities globally, consistently achieving a 98% acceptance rate when applying to renowned colleges and universities. Representatives from worldwide universities regularly visit Discovery School, guiding students through admission procedures, career paths, campus living, accommodations, and financial support. This empowers students to explore options, build connections, and make informed decisions about their preferred university.
